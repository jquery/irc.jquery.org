The jQuery Foundation makes extensive use of Internet Relay Chat for user support and realtime project communication. Getting connected is a great way to get help…or get involved!

Channels

All of our channels are hosted on Freenode. Server: chat.freenode.net. If you've never connected to IRC before, or you're just a bit rusty, take a look at our IRC Help page to get up to speed.

#css-chassis
Support and development chat for users of Chassis. Whether you need help, or you want to help others, or both, this is the place to be.
#globalize
Support and development chat for users of Globalize. Whether you need help, or you want to help others, or both, this is the place to be.
#esprima
Esprima support and development chat.
#esprima-meeting
The Esprima team has weekly public meetings at a scheduled time in this channel.
#grunt
Support chat for users of grunt who need help or want to help others.
#grunt-dev
Chat for grunt development issues.
#jquery
Support chat for users of jQuery, jQuery UI, and jQuery Mobile. Whether you need help, or you want to help others, or both, this is the place to be.
#jquery-content
Discussion of work on jQuery Foundation websites, including content, documentation, and design.
#jquery-dev
jQuery Core development chat.
#jquery-infrastructure
Discussion of work on jQuery Foundation servers, DNS, deployments, and other related infrastructure.
#jquery-meeting
Each team has weekly public meetings at a scheduled time in this channel. The channel is typically +m (moderated) unless there is a meeting taking place.
#jqueryui-dev
jQuery UI and jQuery Mobile development chat.
#pep
Support and development chat for users of PEP. Whether you need help, or you want to help others, or both, this is the place to be.

Logging

Each of our channels is publicly logged to this site. Use the menu on the left to browse each channel's logs.
